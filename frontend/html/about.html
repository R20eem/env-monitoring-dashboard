About Us

The Environmental Monitoring Dashboard is a web application designed to help Upstart farmers who know nothing about farming to monitor their crops and make informed decisions based on real-time data.

Our Goal

Our goal is to empower farmers with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their crops.

Our Team

Our team consists of a group of level 2 Undergrad students from the University of Leeds who are passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems in agriculture.
